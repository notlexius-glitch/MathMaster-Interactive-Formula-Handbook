Pythagorean Identity

sin²(θ) + cos²(θ) = 1

Secant Identity

1 + tan²(θ) = sec²(θ)

Cosecant Identity

1 + cot²(θ) = csc²(θ)

Sine Angle Sum

sin(α + β) = sinα cosβ + cosα sinβ

Sine Angle Difference

sin(α − β) = sinα cosβ − cosα sinβ

Cosine Angle Sum

cos(α + β) = cosα cosβ − sinα sinβ

Cosine Angle Difference

cos(α − β) = cosα cosβ + sinα sinβ

Tangent Angle Sum

tan(α + β) = (tanα + tanβ)/(1 − tanαtanβ)

Tangent Angle Difference

tan(α − β) = (tanα − tanβ)/(1 + tanαtanβ)

Double Angle Sine

sin(2θ) = 2sinθcosθ

Double Angle Cosine

cos(2θ) = cos²θ − sin²θ

Double Angle Tangent

tan(2θ) = 2tanθ / (1 − tan²θ)

Half Angle Sine

sin²θ = (1 − cos(2θ))/2

Half Angle Cosine

cos²θ = (1 + cos(2θ))/2
