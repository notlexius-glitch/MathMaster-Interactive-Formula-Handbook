Distance Formula

√[(x₂ - x₁)² + (y₂ - y₁)²]

Midpoint Formula

((x₁ + x₂)/2 , (y₁ + y₂)/2)

Section Formula

((mx₂ + nx₁)/(m+n), (my₂ + ny₁)/(m+n))

Slope of Line

m = (y₂ - y₁)/(x₂ - x₁)

Equation of Line (Point-Slope)

y - y₁ = m(x - x₁)

Equation of Line (Slope-Intercept)

y = mx + c

General Form of Line

Ax + By + C = 0

Area of Triangle

½ |x₁(y₂ - y₃) + x₂(y₃ - y₁) + x₃(y₁ - y₂)|

Collinearity Condition

Area = 0 ⇒ points are collinear

Distance from Origin

√(x² + y²)
